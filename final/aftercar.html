After Care

First hour after gem application:

  • Do not eat
  • Do not chew any substances (ex:// gum)
  • Do not drink anything except water

Twelve hours in after gem application:

  • Do not touch or play with gem using tongue or fingers
  • Do not brush your teeth
  • Consumption of soft foods are okay
  • Do not chew any substances (ex:// gum)

Additional Information

  • Moving forward use soft bristled tooth brushes to brush your teeth
  • Whitening teeth after gem application may cause your gem to fall off sooner
  • The adhesive used to bond the gems are the same adhesives that dentist use for braces
  • Gem life differs for every individual and depends on how you care for it

It is unlikely for your gem to come off within' the first month of application, as all of our gems have a guarantee of 1 month.

Please notify us if your gem falls off within the first month and we will replace it for FREE!
